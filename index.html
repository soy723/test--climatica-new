Platform for Mega Scale Climate Use Cases.

water is at the heart/earth of everything

In a world facing new climate related emergencies impacting nature, people, businesses and communities, we are developing an advanced analytical and predictive decision-making platform to help address challenges at scale.

How we are making an impact:

SDG #6
clean water and sanitation

One in three people around the world still lack access to safe drinking water. Water is essential for cleanliness, hygiene and purification. Billions of people lack basic hygiene and sanitation due to scarcity of clean water. We aim to enable stakeholders with actionable data to help the maximise the use of this increasingly scarce resource.

How we are making an impact:

SDG #11
sustainable cities and communities

Rapid urbanisation around the world is creating stress on urban environments, their inhabitants, public health and hygiene and the management of sewage and used water. Our platform aims to provide insights at all levels of society to create water resilience and better sustainable usage.

How we are making an impact:

SDG #12
responsible consumption and production

Agriculture is the biggest user of water around the world accounting for approximately 70% of all water resources. Our platform aims to provide attribution data so people can be more mindful and optimise usage of water for their personal, agricultural, economic or industrial needs.

How we are making an impact:

SDG #13
climate action

Floods, drought, heatwaves and forest fires are but some of the few examples of climatic events we increasingly face on a regular basis. Our platform Aims to tie the dots between attribution and consumption. In other words high-level causes and effects of water usage and our climate by providing meaningful data to types of user groups.
